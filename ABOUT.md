# About Falcon Downloader

**Falcon Downloader** is a next-generation download manager designed for speed, security, and versatility. Built with modern C++17/20, it delivers exceptional performance across all major platforms - Windows, macOS, and Linux.

## Our Mission

We believe downloading should be fast, secure, and effortless. Falcon combines cutting-edge technology with an intuitive interface to provide the ultimate downloading experience. Whether you're downloading large files, streaming media, or managing cloud storage, Falcon handles it all with ease.

- **Core**: C++17/20 with modern async programming

- **Build System**: CMake for cross-platform builds

## Open Source Commitment

Falcon is proudly open source under the Apache License 2.0. We believe in transparency, community collaboration, and the power of open-source software. Our vibrant community of developers and users continuously contributes to making Falcon better every day.

## Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our top priority. Falcon never collects personal data or tracks your downloads. All credentials are encrypted locally with industry-standard AES-256 encryption, ensuring your cloud storage accounts remain secure.
